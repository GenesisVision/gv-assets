Description:
Private Instant Verified Transaction, PIVX, is a privacy focused decentralized open source cryptocurrency launched on Feb 1, 2016, under the name of Darknet (DNET) before it was professionally re-branded to PIVX. The main goal of PIVX is to achieve near instant private transactions and a governance that helps sustain the network for the benefit of all of the users involved.
