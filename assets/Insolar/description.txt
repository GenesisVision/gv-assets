Description:
Insolar is a global technology company building innovative public and private blockchain solutions on Insolar Blockchain Platform, the most secure, flexible and scalable blockchain for business. The Insolar ERC-20 token (INS) is currently listed on Binance, Bithumb, OKex and a number of other exchanges. At the moment Insolar has announced collaborations with: Microsoft and Oracle - to make Insolar available on Microsoft Azure and Oracle Cloud marketplaces; Innosuisse - the Swiss Innovation Agency; UC Berkeley for an accelerator program and joint projects; and BiTA - Blockchain in Transport Alliance and Cointelegraph Consulting as the launch partner.
