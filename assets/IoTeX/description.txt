IoTeX began the journey in 2017 to build the Internet of Trusted Things, where all physical and virtual “things” — humans, machines, businesses, and dApps — can efficiently exchange information and value at the global scale. IoTeX combines auto-scalable blockchain, trusted hardware and edge computing to realize the full potential of IoT.
