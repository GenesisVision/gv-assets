Description:
Yee is a blockchain-powered & cloud-based social ecosystem. Yee project includes 4 parts: 
1. YeeChain: a blockchain supporting fast transaction and high-efficiency storage;
2. YeeNet: a cloud-based communication network based on YeeChain;
3. Typical applications: YeeCall, YeeWallet, Dapps (decentralized applications), and YeeStore; 
4. Yee ecosystem.
