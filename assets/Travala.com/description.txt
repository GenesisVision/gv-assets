Description:
Founded in 2017, Travala.com claims to be the leading blockchain-based travel booking platform offering over 2,000,000 hotels and accommodations in 230 countries with cheaper prices than mainstream travel booking platforms. As the native cryptocurrency of Travala.com, AVA is a Binance Chain-based token that can be used for payments, givebacks, loyalty rewards, activating discounts, a store of value and several other use cases.
