Description:
Nexus (NXS) is an open source blockchain and peer-to-peer network. The project aims to 'develop technology for communities, enterprise, and connectivity'. As of 1 Mar 2019, Nexus comprises 3 Embassies in the US, UK, and Australia. Each of the Nexus Embassies is a non-profit entity created for technological/business development, along with education. The nonprofit entities have no owners, are represented by a volunteer board of directors, and are operated by teams of creatives and delegates.
