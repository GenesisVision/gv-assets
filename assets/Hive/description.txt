Description:
Hive is a Graphene-based social blockchain (and community hard-fork of STEEM) that launched on 03/20/2020 with the core idea of decentralization. Hive's prime selling points are its true decentralization, speed, and scalability. Other notable features include it having the lowest entry-barrier for user adoption in the market, time delay security, integrated token allocation, and the upcoming Smart Media Token integration. It is an ideal building location for a variety of innovative projects focused on a broad range of fields, from open source development to social games. Hive aims to be the preferred blockchain for dApp development.
