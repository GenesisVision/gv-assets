Description:
Quoine Liquid is a trading platform that aims to combine every liquidity source into a single highly liquid tradable order book. The Quoine Liquid team will empower their services with the blockchain technology and consequently allow the migration of financial institutions to a decentralized platform. QASH is an ERC-20 token that will allow users to benefit from the platform services and also work as a trading asset.
